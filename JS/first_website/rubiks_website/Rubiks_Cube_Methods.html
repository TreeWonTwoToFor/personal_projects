Different Rubik's Cube Methods

There are many ways to solve a Rubik's Cube, these are called methods. Methods may be very different, or pretty similar. Some involve many algorithms, some are very simple to learn, and some are fast if you practice.

CFOP

CFOP, or Cross F2L OLL and PLL is a very well known speedcubing method. It is hard to learn, but only needs about 5 algorithms per solve.

Roux

Roux is a method where you build 2 1x2x3 blocks and place the other corners and edges and centers in the right position.

ZZ/ZZ-CT

ZZ has 3 steps, EOLine, ZZ F2L, and LL, it is not as well known, but can be very fast. It has a LOT of algorithms. ZZ-CT is basically ZZ, but fixes some problems with ZZ.

Beginners Method

One of the easiest methods, easy to learn, but not very fast, minimal algorithms to learn.
